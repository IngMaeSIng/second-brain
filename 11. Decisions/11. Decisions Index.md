> ADRs — cross-functional decisions with rationale.

## What lives here

One note per decision that crosses domains or has lasting consequences (pricing, hiring, repositioning). Records the context, the call, and why. Use the Decision template. When a new decision replaces an old one, mark the old `status: superseded`.
